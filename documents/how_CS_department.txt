Hi all! I'm very interested in attending UNR and had a few questions, I guess the main one being about the CS department. Can anyone shed a little light to how it is and how the professors are? Thank you so much!

CS major here and enjoying it. Professors vary. Erin Keith is a good intro prof while Papachristos can be much tougher. Most are good though, you will learn how to pass a class while also learning the material (read: figure out how to get the A). Your best resource are the TAs and your fellow students. We have Discord servers set up for our classes. Additionally, most students you meet in these classes will end up with very similar or the same schedule as you, especially in later semesters, and they become a strong backbone when it comes to your success.
